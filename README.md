Objective:
The goal of this project was to analyze and visualize product sales and shipment performance across multiple countries to provide actionable insights for business decision-making.

Tools Used:
Power BI, Excel (sample-chocolate-sales-data-all.xlsx)

Project Summary:
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze global chocolate product sales and shipment data. The dashboard provides an in-depth view of sales performance by product, shipment ID, and region, helping stakeholders monitor business growth and operational efficiency.

Key features of the dashboard include:

Sales Analysis: Bar chart showing Sum of Amount by Product, identifying top-performing products such as Manuka Honey & Pure Dark Choco and Raspberry Choco.

Shipment Distribution: Donut chart displaying Category count by SPID, revealing balanced shipment volumes across all shipment IDs (SP22â€“SP25).

Order Tracking: Interactive filters for Year, Quarter, Month, Geo, and Order Status to view shipment details by country and time period.

Regional Performance: Highlighted New Zealand as a key performing region with consistently delivered orders.

Data-Driven Decisions: Enabled insights into revenue contribution, shipment efficiency, and category performance for better supply chain and sales management.

Outcome:
The dashboard streamlined data interpretation for the management team, providing a single, interactive source of truth for tracking revenue trends and delivery performance across countries. It supports quick decision-making, helps identify top-selling products, and ensures shipment balance.
